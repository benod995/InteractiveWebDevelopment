Brief History About Us

Here at Ben's Pizza Place, we have managed to perfect our pizza so that everybody, even people who resent pizza can enjoy it. I (Ben) Opened up this place in March 2014, after traveling the world reviewing and working in various resteraunts, tasting different cousines and shaddowing some of the best chefs not just in Italy, everywhere! From Denmark to Pakistan I have seen it all. I decided in 2010 that I wanted to open up my own resteraunt and saw a gap in the market for a really good quality pizza place where you can sit down with your family or friends or get it delivered to your front door. I searched for the best chefs internationlly and am very proud of what we have accomplished as a team. We are very keen on maintaining a high standard of food and encourage everyone to try it out. We also give great discounts!
